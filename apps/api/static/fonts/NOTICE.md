# Bundled fonts

Each font here ships with its licence. The two families under the SIL Open Font
Licence 1.1 share the full text in `OFL.txt`; their copyright statements are
below, alongside the terms for the one font that is not under the OFL.

- SIL Open Font Licence 1.1 (see `OFL.txt`)

## Bebas Neue - Regular
- Copyright (c) 2010 by Dharma Type (https://github.com/dharmatype/Bebas-Neue)
- SIL Open Font Licence 1.1 (see `OFL.txt`)

## Trivial - Regular, Bold, Heavy, ExtraLight, UltraLight
- By Yeah Noah, obtained from https://www.dafont.com/trivial.font (labelled
  "100% Free" there).
- The font grants free use in its own metadata. Its `name` table reads:
  "Copyleft by Yeah Noah. All lefts reversed.", "Trivial is not a trademark of
  Yeah Noah.", and "Every type of use allowed."
- The author reaffirmed this in a comment on the font's dafont.com page
  (21 January 2023): "Yes, Trivial font family is free for any type of use,
  even commercial."
